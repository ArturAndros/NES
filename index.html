Технологии

Unity - это игровой движок, который позволит вашему ребенку создавать игры под большинство популярных платформ. С помощью данного движка разрабатываются игры, запускающиеся на персональных компьютерах (работающих под Windows, MacOS, Linux), на смартфонах и планшетах (iOS, Android, Windows Phone), на игровых консолях (PS, Xbox, Wii).

О игре

TIC TAC TOE

Overview

Endless Runner game based on 80 years style, to bring user back to past and feel nostalgic. The main concept this game is a car drift, use turbo, match coins and upgrade your car with new engine and new details.

Description

In a game you can buy new cars, upgrade, paint and also modify details. When you drive, your details is breaking and after breaking one of them, you cannot drive on and when it"s critical damage, your car is exploding, but you can repair it. Every car and detail has moving characteristics. The main currency is lightnings, you can exchange golds to lightnings.

Technology stack

Unity 3D/C#/Native + Android Studio/Xcode/Swift
